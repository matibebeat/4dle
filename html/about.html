Work

Mathis has written the home page while Maxime wrote the index and about page. However, the two of us have developed the goal of the website which is a "guess the member of the class" game.

Game

Hello nerds ! Maybe you know "Wordle" or "loLdle", the online games where you need to guess respectively a word and a champion from the game "League of Legends" ? Well, here is a new game you'll be able to play during class :D ! You have to try to guess a student of the class based on its characteristics : their hair and eye color, their ranking in the class (#/21), their favorite activity, of they are right or left handed, if they are a gamer or not and their height. Good luck and have fun !!!
