What is 4dle ?

Hello nerds ! Maybe you know "Wordle" or "loLdle", the online games where you need to guess respectively a word and a champion from the game "League of Legends" ?
Well, here is a new game you'll be able to play during class :D !4dle is a game where you have to guess the name of a student in the class based on their characteristics. We have 7 characteristics : their hair and eye color, their ranking in the class (#/21), their favorite activity, their dominant hand, their height and if they are a gamer or not.
